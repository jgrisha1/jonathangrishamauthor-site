Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This website is operated by Jonathan Grisham. This policy explains what data this site collects, how it is used, and what your rights are.

What this site does not do: This site does not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking or advertising cookies. No cookies are set by this site itself.

Google Fonts: This site loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com). When your browser requests these fonts, Google's servers receive your IP address and browser information. This is standard behavior for any site using Google Fonts. Google's privacy policy governs that data: policies.google.com/privacy.

Forms: If you submit the contact form, signed-copy request form, or mailing list form on this site, your name, email address, and any details you include are sent directly to Jonathan Grisham. Your data is used only to respond to your inquiry or fulfill your request and is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Amazon links: This site links to Amazon product pages. Amazon may set cookies when you follow those links. Amazon's privacy policy governs that data: amazon.com/privacy.

Hosting: This site is hosted on Cloudflare Workers. Cloudflare may collect standard server log data including IP addresses. Cloudflare's privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Your rights: If you have submitted a form and wish to request deletion of your data, contact Jonathan directly via the contact form on this site with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."

Children: This site contains extreme horror content and is not intended for anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy: This policy may be updated occasionally. The date at the top of the page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact: Questions about this policy can be submitted via the contact form on this site.
